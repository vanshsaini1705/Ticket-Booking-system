About RCERT

Rajkiya College of Engineering & Research Technology (RCERT) is a premier educational institution committed to excellence in technical education. Our annual Garba Night celebration brings together students, faculty, and the community to celebrate the rich cultural heritage of Gujarat.

Join us for an unforgettable evening of traditional dance, music, and celebration as we honor our cultural roots while building lasting memories.
